# 🧠 GEPEA Psychology Research Group Website

This repository contains the source code for the official website of **GEPEA**, a Psychology research group dedicated to promoting academic research, scientific dissemination, and community engagement.

The website was developed to provide information about the group's mission, research projects, members, publications, events, and academic activities.

---

## 🌟 About the Project

The GEPEA website serves as a digital platform to connect researchers, students, and the community with the group's work.

Its primary objectives are:

- Present the research group's mission and objectives.
- Showcase ongoing and completed research projects.
- Share scientific publications and academic materials.
- Introduce the research team and collaborators.
- Promote events, workshops, and academic initiatives.
- Facilitate communication between the group and the public.
